ABOUT US



Hope you are enjoying as you are travelling through the website.

We the students of the IIIT Hyderabad are the creators of this socialising website where u can find likeminded people as yours!!!!!!




SOCIALISING

Socialisng is an important part of studying at IIIT Hyderabad. Friends play an important role in life. But as we know, many students face issues with socialising with their roommates fearing to talk with them because of many internal factors.

We always want someone to be with us, who understands us, listens to our intrests and joins us actively in the conversation. Not only that having relation with like minded people increases our confidence and helps in staying happy. Therefore this website is a platforms to such kind of students who are eagerly searching for a friend or friends of their type............

HOSTEL

Hostel life is full of opportunities and excitement. One can make friends with fellow hostellers of different tastes and knowledge which can be helpful in studying as well. One may choose friends of his own likes and interests and can spend quality time with them even when classes end.

Hostellers can get to know each other very well by sharing their interests and things, this builds a strong relationship between fellow mates and will build unity among them.







STRESS RELIVERS

Playing with friends will be the most fun activity to do in the college life which removes our stress caused due to studies. Having a person known of your taste of sport will help you to mingle well and play and enjoy the sport at the same time.

And one can always teach one another about playing even better. Our app helps in connecting the hobbies of various students and make a bond between them.
